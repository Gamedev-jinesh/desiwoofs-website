About "DESI WOOFS"

Our flagship project, Desi Woofs, is a 3D narrative adventure game inspired by Life is Strange 2, designed to raise awareness and funds for the welfare of Indian street dogs. Through engaging multiplayer gameplay and a culturally rich modern Indian cityscape, the game explores the daily struggles of street dogs with humour, empathy, and Bollywood-style storytelling.

Game Concept

Desi Woofs offers a unique blend of gameplay mechanics that encourage players to think creatively and act compassionately. As they navigate the bustling streets and hidden corners of Indian cities, players must use their chosen dog's special abilities to solve environmental puzzles and overcome obstacles. Each dog's skills are rooted in their cultural background, adding an educational layer to the experience.

Whether it's deciphering clues in a crowded market or outsmarting a mischievous monkey, players are constantly engaged in tasks that highlight teamwork and problem-solving.

Co-Op Adventure

In this delightful Co-Op multiplayer experience, 2 players step into the paws of street-smart, quirky dogs, each with big hearts and even bigger personalities. Players can select their preferred dog type, each showcasing unique skills, backgrounds, and voices influenced by various Indian dialects and cultural nuances. By night, they become part of the Desi Woofs gang—a brave pack dedicated to protecting and supporting street dogs struggling to survive.

Games for Good

GameChangersIndia is more than just gaming; it's also about promoting "Games for Good." We inspire developers to create games for charitable causes, like "A game for street dogs in India," where the proceeds can be used to improve the lives of street dogs in the country.

Making a Real-World Impact:

  • • Global Collaboration: Partnering with NGOs worldwide to support street dogs and other animals in need.
  • • Direct Contribution: Donating $2 from every Steam sale to animal welfare charities.
  • • Real Change: Funding rescue missions, medical care, and shelter programs across the globe.
  • • A Movement, Not Just a Game: Every player becomes a part of a global effort to help animals.
